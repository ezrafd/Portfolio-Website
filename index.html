About

I am a student at Garfield High School in Seattle, Washington and I work at the best pizza place in town, Pagliacci Pizza. I am also the co-clerk of North Pacfic Yearly Meeting Junior Friends, a tight knit community of young Quakers.

I am focused on exploring as much of life as possible and having the hard conversations we need to foster community and understanding. As the founder of data4humanity, my current mission is to educate anyone and everyone I can about technology ethics and algorithmic accountability. I am looking to grow not only my understanding of the world and my perspective, but my impact on my community and the world.
